# Chat2Close: The AI Sales Operating System for WhatsApp

## 1. Executive Summary
**Chat2Close** is an all-in-one AI sales platform designed to turn WhatsApp into a revenue-generating machine. Unlike standard chatbots that just answer FAQs, Chat2Close is a "Sales Agent" that actively negotiates, upsells, and closes deals. It combines a Unified Inbox, CRM, Order Management, and Marketing Automation into a single dashboard, specifically optimized for high-growth markets (including Africa) with integrations for local payments like Paystack and Flutterwave.

## 5. How to Connect & Setup

### Step 1: Connect WhatsApp (Twilio)
1.  Go to **Settings > Connect**.
2.  Enter your **Twilio Account SID** and **Auth Token**.
3.  Select your Twilio Phone Number.
4.  *Status changes to "Connected".*

### Step 2: Configure AI Brain
1.  Go to **Settings > AI Configuration**.
2.  Enter your **Google Gemini API Key**.
3.  Set your **System Prompt** (e.g., "You are a helpful assistant for [Business Name]...").
4.  Upload your **Knowledge Base** (PDFs, text) so the AI knows your return policies and FAQs.

### Step 3: Setup Payments
1.  Go to **Settings > Finance**.
2.  Enable **Paystack** or **Flutterwave**.
3.  Enter your Public/Secret Keys.
4.  *Now you can generate payment links directly in chat.*
